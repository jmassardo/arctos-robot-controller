## 📊 Feature Prioritization Matrix (RICE Analysis)

### Methodology

**RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort**

- **Reach**: Number of users affected per quarter (1-4 scale)
- **Impact**: Business impact per user (1-3 scale: minimal, moderate, high)
- **Confidence**: Certainty in estimates (0.5-1.0 scale: 50%-100%)
- **Effort**: Development time in person-months (actual estimate)

### Backlog Grooming Guidelines

#### Story Refinement Process

1. **Story Creation**: Product Manager creates high-level stories based on user
   research
2. **Technical Refinement**: Development team adds technical details and
   estimates
3. **Acceptance Criteria Review**: Product Manager and QA define precise
   acceptance criteria
4. **Story Point Estimation**: Team uses planning poker for collaborative
   estimation
5. **Definition of Done Review**: Ensure all stories meet DoD criteria

#### Story Quality Checklist

- [ ] **Independent**: Story can be developed independently
- [ ] **Negotiable**: Details can be adjusted during implementation
- [ ] **Valuable**: Provides clear business or user value
- [ ] **Estimable**: Team can estimate effort with reasonable confidence
- [ ] **Small**: Can be completed within a sprint
- [ ] **Testable**: Acceptance criteria enable verification

#### Backlog Maintenance

- **Weekly Grooming**: Review and refine top 20 stories
- **Sprint Planning**: Select stories for upcoming sprint based on priority and
  capacity
- **Story Updates**: Maintain stories based on user feedback and changing
  requirements
- **Technical Debt**: Allocate 20% of capacity to technical debt and
  infrastructure
